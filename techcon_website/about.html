History

The tech conference, which began in 2018, was founded with the goal of bringing together tech enthusiasts, developers, and industry leaders to foster innovation and collaboration. Since its inception, the conference has grown into a prominent event in Nairobi, attracting both local and international participants. Over the years, it has provided a platform for learning, networking, and sharing ideas, with renowned developers and experts leading discussions on cutting-edge technologies. The conference has played a significant role in nurturing the tech ecosystem in Nairobi, offering valuable opportunities for attendees to stay ahead of trends and expand their professional connections.

Mission

The mission of this tech conference is to empower and inspire the tech community by providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and networking. We aim to connect tech enthusiasts, developers, and industry leaders, fostering innovation and knowledge sharing to drive the growth of technology in Nairobi and beyond. Through engaging sessions and hands-on experiences, we strive to cultivate an environment where ideas can thrive, and individuals can develop the skills and networks needed to succeed in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
